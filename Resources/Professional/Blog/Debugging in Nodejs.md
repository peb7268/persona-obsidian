	<h1>Debugging in Nodejs</h1>
	<div class="post-content">
		&lt;div class&#x3D;&quot;kg-card-markdown&quot;&gt;&lt;p&gt;To debug you can do a few things:&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;ol&gt;
&lt;li&gt;insert console.logs&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;Look at the stack trace&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;run &lt;code&gt;node debug &amp;lt;script_name&amp;gt;&lt;/code&gt;.&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;or use the chrome dev tools with node-inspector.&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;/ol&gt;
&lt;h2 id&#x3D;&quot;usingnodeinspector&quot;&gt;Using Node-Inspector&lt;/h2&gt;
&lt;p&gt;To use node-inspector ( NI ) you have to do a few steps:&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;ul&gt;
&lt;li&gt;Install it globally.&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;open your app in debug mode &lt;code&gt;node --debug appname&lt;/code&gt;&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;run your app via the regular way&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;Open up your app in the browser&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;in a seperate tab open your window to the specified pot.&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;/ul&gt;
