&lt;p&gt;The Googster breaks down page activity into three main buckets. Users, (who interacts w your site), sessions, ( what they do during their visit until they are innactive for 30 min. ), and interactions (They click and do stuff).&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;h2 id&#x3D;&quot;campaigntags&quot;&gt;Campaign Tags&lt;/h2&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Source: Where they came from. (Google, somesite.com, ect.. )&lt;br&gt;
Medium: What type of referral. (Organic, Referral, or none which is direct)&lt;br&gt;
Campaign: what the name of the campaign is&lt;br&gt;
Term: Keyword for paid search term&lt;br&gt;
content: allows you to differentiate between 2 versions of 1 ad.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;These attributes can be overridden with link tags using the &lt;a href&#x3D;&quot;https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/1033867&quot;&gt;Url Builder&lt;/a&gt; from Google.&lt;/p&gt;
